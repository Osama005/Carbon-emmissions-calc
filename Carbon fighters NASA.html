How it works

  1. You have to input the information about your area according to your the fields below as required.

  2. If you get any trouble you can use the links under the text field to know information and then write the estimation.

  3. Check that all the boxes is written with the right number and all of them is full in the same time.

  4. Click the button submit and then the estimation for the carbon emission in your area will appear.
